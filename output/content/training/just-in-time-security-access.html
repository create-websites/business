    Strategic Roadmap for JIT Implementation

    A successful JIT program is a journey, not a destination. Follow this phased approach to maximize security gains while minimizing disruption.

    Phase 1: Plan & Discover

    Start with your "crown jewels." Identify the highest-risk accounts and systems. Use discovery tools to map existing permissions, find over-privileged accounts, and establish a baseline for your JIT policies.

    1
    2

    Phase 2: Execute & Automate

    Deploy your chosen solution with a relentless focus on user experience. Automate approval workflows for low-risk requests and integrate deeply with ChatOps (Slack, Teams) and ITSM tools to make access seamless.

    Phase 3: Maintain & Refine

    JIT is not "set and forget." Continuously audit logs, solicit user feedback to reduce friction, and provide ongoing training. Use the data to refine policies and detect anomalies, evolving your security posture.
