Risks & Challenges

Despite rapid growth, the ecosystem faces significant hurdles regarding security, interoperability, and supply chain resilience.

Challenge Area Severity Description Mitigation Trend
Legacy OT Security High Unpatched machinery connected to networks vulnerable to ransomware. Network segmentation & Air-gapping.
E-Waste Medium Billions of disposable sensors creating environmental hazards. Biodegradable PCBs & Battery-less tech.
Spectrum Congestion Low Interference in 2.4GHz bands due to device density. Migration to 6GHz (Wi-Fi 6E/7).
Privacy Regs Medium GDPR/CCPA compliance for personal data on smart devices. Data minimization strategies.
