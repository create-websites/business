The Atelier Process

From Bean to Bonbon

I
🌱

Sourcing

We select only the top 5% of cacao harvests from our three partner farms, tasting each lot before purchase.

II
🔥

Roasting

Small-batch roasting at precise temperatures unlock the unique tasting notes of each origin — never masked, always celebrated.

III
🍫

Tempering

Hand-tempered chocolate gives every piece its characteristic snap, sheen, and melt. A skill mastered over years of practice.

IV
🎨

Finishing

Hand-painted with cocoa butter, dusted, enrobed, or dipped — each piece finished individually by our team of artisans.
