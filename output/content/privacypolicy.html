1) Information We Collect

Information you provide: If you contact us using the email link on the Site, we receive the information you choose to include in your message (such as your name, email address, and the message content).

Information collected automatically: When you visit the Site, information may be collected automatically through standard server logs and Google Analytics, such as:

  • IP address

  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, referring/exit pages

2) Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site (for example, which pages are visited most) so we can improve content and site performance. Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies and collect information such as IP address and information about your device/browser and activity on the Site.

Google may process this information according to its own policies. You can control cookies through your browser settings, and you may also be able to manage certain privacy settings in your Google account.

3) Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies for advertising or marketing, and we do not use tracking pixels for cross-site behavioral advertising. However, Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies to measure and report usage statistics.

4) How We Use Information

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above. Emails you send us may be retained to respond and keep appropriate records. Google Analytics data is retained according to the settings configured in our Google Analytics account.
