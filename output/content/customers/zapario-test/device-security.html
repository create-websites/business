Incident Response

The ROI of Secure OTA

When a zero-day vulnerability is discovered, time is your enemy. Without robust Over-The-Air (OTA) update infrastructure, fleets remain exposed for months while manual "truck rolls" (dispatching technicians) are coordinated at massive expense.

  • Rapid Mitigation

    Patch critical flaws globally in hours, not months.

  • Fail-Safe Rollbacks

    Dual-bank memory architectures ensure a device reverts to previous working firmware if an update is corrupted, preventing "bricking".
