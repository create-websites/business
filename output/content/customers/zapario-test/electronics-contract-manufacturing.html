Zero Defect Philosophy

Multi-Gate Quality Assurance

Quality cannot be inspected *into* a product at the end of the line; it must be built in. We utilize a multi-gate inspection process to catch and correct anomalies at the source.

  • SPI & 3D AOI

    Solder Paste Inspection and Automated Optical Inspection ensure perfect volumetric solder deposits and component placement.

  • X-Ray (AXI)

    Essential for verifying hidden solder joints on BGA, QFN, and LGA packages.

  • ICT & FCT

    In-Circuit Testing checks individual component values, while Functional Testing verifies the board operates to specification.
