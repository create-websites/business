Our Collaborative Process

We follow a proven four-step method to ensure stunning results and client satisfaction.

1

Quick Intake (10 min)

Share your business basics (services, location, customers, goals), plus any brand assets. If you don’t have content yet, that’s fine—our AI will generate a strong first draft.

2

AI Content First (≈90% automated)

Our LLM agent turns your inputs into the right “story” and layout: sitemap, page flow, section outlines, headlines, CTAs, FAQs—then auto-generates a clean, conversion-focused design to match.

3

Human Polish + Launch (≈10% hands-on)

We refine the final details that matter: accuracy, tone, trust elements, visuals, mobile responsiveness, speed checks, and launch

4

Ongoing AI Updates

On a regular schedule (weekly/monthly), the AI agent keeps your site fresh: SEO improvements, new content sections/pages
