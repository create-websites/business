What is Demand Sensing?

It's the art and science of listening to the market in near real-time. Instead of just relying on past sales, demand sensing tunes into a wide variety of digital signals to create highly accurate short-term forecasts. For the food industry, this means less waste, fuller shelves, and happier customers.

By understanding subtle shifts in consumer behavior as they happen, companies can adjust their supply chains dynamically.

40% Reduction in Forecast Errors

Companies using demand sensing can drastically improve their forecast accuracy for the upcoming 1-4 weeks.
