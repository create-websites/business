Sessions

Choose from private or small-group Pilates sessions. All sessions are tailored, technique-led, and adaptable for all experience levels.

Private 1:1 Pilates

One-to-one sessions designed around your body, goals, and movement history.

  • Highly personalised programming
  • Reformer and mat work
  • Ideal for beginners, rehab-aware training, or accelerated progress

Private sessions allow focused attention on alignment, breath, and control, with progressions introduced at the right pace.

  • Posture and core strength
  • Mobility and injury-aware modifications
  • Confidence-building instruction

Small Group Pilates

Structured small-group sessions with individual guidance in a supportive environment.

  • Limited group sizes
  • Progressive weekly programming
  • Motivating and social setting

Small-group sessions are ideal for maintaining consistency while still receiving attentive coaching.

  • Strength, posture, and mobility focus
  • Options for mixed and beginner levels
  • Clear cues and controlled pacing
