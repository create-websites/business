Understanding the "House Edge"

Before you place a chip, you must understand the math. The House Edge is the mathematical advantage the casino has over you over time.

You cannot beat the house edge in the long run. A "Pro" optimizes their play to minimize this edge, extending their playtime, earning "comps" (free drinks/rooms), and maximizing the fun of variance (short-term luck).

The Roulette Wheel

Common Myths Busted

‚úï Myth: "The Machine is Due"

Reality: Every spin is independent (RNG). A slot machine doesn't know what happened 5 seconds ago.

‚úï Myth: "Hot & Cold Dealers"

Reality: Superstition. Dealer luck is just variance. Don't blame the dealer for bad cards.

‚úì Truth: "The Players Card"

Reality: Always use it. It tracks your "Coin In" (volume of play) to give you free rooms and food. It does NOT affect the game odds.
